Zero Conditional

Usage: For facts or things that are always true.
Per fets o coses que sempre són veritat.
Tense Structure: If + Present Simple, Present Simple
Estructura verbal: Si + Present Simple, Present Simple
  • Affirmative: "If people migrate, they adapt to new cultures."
  • Positiva: "Si la gent migra, s'adapta a noves cultures."
  • Negative: "If communities don't embrace diversity, tensions arise."
  • Negativa: "Si les comunitats no abracen la diversitat, sorgeixen tensions."
  • Question: "What happens if people move to a new country?"
  • Pregunta: "Què passa si la gent es trasllada a un nou país?"

First Conditional

Usage: For real possibilities in the future.
Per a possibilitats reals en el futur.
Tense Structure: If + Present Simple, will + Base Form
Estructura verbal: Si + Present Simple, will + Forma Base
  • Affirmative: "If I learn Spanish, I'll communicate better when I move to Spain."
  • Positiva: "Si aprenc espanyol, em comunicaré millor quan em mudi a Espanya."
  • Negative: "If we don't address climate change, more people will be forced to migrate."
  • Negativa: "Si no abordem el canvi climàtic, més gent es veurà obligada a migrar."
  • Question: "Will you migrate if you get that job offer abroad?"
  • Pregunta: "Migraràs si reps aquella oferta de treball a l'estranger?"

Second Conditional

Usage: For unreal or hypothetical situations now or in the future.
Per a situacions irreals o hipotètiques en el present o futur.
Tense Structure: If + Past Simple, would + Base Form
Estructura verbal: Si + Past Simple, would + Forma Base
  • Affirmative: "If I had a visa, I would move to Canada."
  • Positiva: "Si tingués un visat, em mudaria al Canadà."
  • Negative: "If globalization didn't exist, we wouldn't enjoy international cuisines."
  • Negativa: "Si la globalització no existís, no gaudiríem de cuines internacionals."
  • Question: "What would you do if you lived in another country?"
  • Pregunta: "Què faries si visquessis en un altre país?"

Third Conditional

Usage: For unreal situations in the past.
Per a situacions irreals en el passat.
Tense Structure: If + Past Perfect, would have + Past Participle
Estructura verbal: Si + Past Perfect, would have + Participi Passat
  • Affirmative: "If she had studied international law, she would have worked at the UN."
  • Positiva: "Si ella hagués estudiat dret internacional, hauria treballat a l'ONU."
  • Negative: "If they hadn't migrated, they wouldn't have experienced new cultures."
  • Negativa: "Si no haguessin migrat, no haurien experimentat noves cultures."
  • Question: "What would have happened if you hadn't moved abroad?"
  • Pregunta: "Què hauria passat si no t'haguessis mudat a l'estranger?"

Mixed Conditional

Usage: Combines past and present situations.
Combina situacions passades i presents.
Tense Structure: If + Past Perfect, would + Base Form
Estructura verbal: Si + Past Perfect, would + Forma Base
  • Affirmative: "If I had saved money, I would be traveling now."
  • Positiva: "Si hagués estalviat diners, estaria viatjant ara."
  • Negative: "If he hadn't left his country, he wouldn't be bilingual now."
  • Negativa: "Si no hagués deixat el seu país, no seria bilingüe ara."
  • Question: "If you had accepted that job overseas, where would you be living now?"
  • Pregunta: "Si haguessis acceptat aquella feina a l'estranger, on estaries vivint ara?"
Summary: Conditionals help us talk about possibilities, imaginations, and consequences related to globalization and migration.
Resum: Els condicionals ens ajuden a parlar de possibilitats, imaginacions i conseqüències relacionades amb la globalització i la migració.
